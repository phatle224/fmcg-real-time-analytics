Data Engineer
About the role
As a Data Engineer, you will be involved in building and operating Vinamilk’s end-to-end data platform. By working closely with data-driven projects, you will design, implement, and optimize infrastructure for ETL/ELT, Data Warehouse, Analytics, and Machine Learning platforms. Your goal is to ensure a stable, efficient, and secure data ecosystem that empowers data-driven decision-making.

Key Responsibilities
Data Platform Architecture & Development:
Design and build the Data Platform, including compute, storage, orchestration, metadata, observability, and security layers.
Deploy, configure, and optimize systems such as Airflow, dbt, ClickHouse OSS, Trino, S3/Lakehouse, Cube.js OSS, Kafka, OpenMetadata, OpenLineage, and related components.
Build or customize platform services and components to support ETL/ELT, data governance, lineage, data quality, metric layer, and company-specific requirements.
Design and operate Data Warehouse/Lakehouse platforms, including partitioning, storage layout, performance tuning, and cost optimization.
Data Pipeline & ML Platform Enablement:
Build ingestion and streaming systems to synchronize data between applications and the data platform (e.g., CDC, event streaming, API pipelines).
Build or support the development of ML platforms, including feature stores, model registries, training pipelines, and batch/stream inference systems.
Platform Operations & Continuous Improvement:
Build observability and monitoring systems, including logs, metrics, alerting, data quality checks, lineage, and SLA/SLO management.
Manage platform lifecycle operations such as backup/restore, scaling, upgrades, security, and access control.
Collaborate with ETL Engineers, Data Analysts, Data Scientists, and backend teams to ensure data is effectively integrated, managed, and utilized.
Propose and implement improvements to enhance system stability, performance, and scalability.
Job Requirements
Professional Background & Knowledge:
Strong understanding of modern data stack technologies such as Kafka, Airflow, dbt, ClickHouse, Doris, Spark, Flink, Cube, S3/Lake storage, OpenMetadata, OpenLineage, Grafana/Prometheus, or equivalent.
Experience in building or customizing services using programming languages such as Python, Go, or Java.
Familiarity with REST/gRPC, message queues, caching mechanisms, and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
Soft skills:
Strong engineering mindset with the ability to design scalable systems and reusable frameworks across multiple teams.
Fast learner with strong adaptability in dynamic and evolving environments.
Mindset & Attitude:
Detail-oriented: Ensures accuracy, stability, and reliability in system design and operations.
Growth mindset: Continuously learns new technologies and improves professional capabilities.
Proactive: Actively identifies issues, proposes solutions, and drives system improvements.
